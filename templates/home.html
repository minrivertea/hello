Stop fucking saying HELLO to me

A story of life in modern China

In 2007 I moved to China to learn Mandarin and experience life in one of the world's most interesting cultures.

I've been here for 4 years now, and I live happily with my wife and my cat Oni!

* some of the naivety has gone, although I still get starry-eyed when I see an old man doing kung fu in the parks.

But there's just one SMALL thing that still pisses me off...

WHY?

Some say it's because Chinese people are so friendly, and others say it's because Chinese people are so racist. My own simple idea is that a large number of Chinese people have never been told that this isn't polite.

WHO?

People shouting HELLO are young and old, rich and poor. I've had it from uneducated manual labourers, and also from businessmen and university students who are studying English! It's worst in 2nd tier cities actually.

After months of this, playing on your nerves every day, every foreigner loses it and has what can only be described as a...foreigner tantrum

If you hadn't guessed it, this website is my little foreigner tantrum*.

GRAARRHGHGGGH!!

* My tantrum started off this morning. As I was stretching in the park, a Chinese man walked past. Without even looking at me or stopping in his stride, he shouted a full blast HELLO, quickly followed by a FUCK YOU. I get something like this at least once a day in China, but today it just pissed me off more than usual.

You might think I'm overreacting - I agree with you! But it's amazingly frustrating, every time you leave the house or try to do anything, and some random stranger shouts HEEELLLLOOOOOO at you. Come to China, try it for yourself and see how long you last before you have your first foreigner tantrum. Most people's upper limit is a couple of months.

Anyway China, because we're friends, I have a solution!

Let's build better international relations through this simple 2-step guide to globally recognised, totally acceptable ways to say HELLO. Unless of course you are just genuinely racist, you'll quickly become an expert in how NOT to offend people you meet.

1 Saying HELLO is considered rude in these situations.

2 This is the correct way to say HELLO to people.


  • Face me when you say it
  • Don't shout
  • Say it in your normal voice
  • Try smiling
  • If you can't speak English, use Chinese
    (yes, some of us can speak Chinese too)
  • Don't laugh or snigger after you've said it
  • Don't run away after you've said it
  • Don't send your 2yr old kid over to say it
